## Full Description
Igbo Calendar is a comprehensive cultural reference app that bridges the traditional Igbo calendar system with the modern Gregorian calendar.

**Key Features:**
- Full Igbo calendar with authentic month and day names
- 4-day Igbo week cycle: Eke, Orie, Afọ, Nkwọ
- Market day tracker — know when and where markets are open
- Cultural festivals and events calendar
- Side-by-side Gregorian and Igbo date view
- Date search and navigation across years
- Two beautiful themes: Amber (gold) and Forest Green
- Works fully offline — no internet required

Whether you're Igbo, studying the culture, or just curious about one of Africa's oldest calendar traditions, this app keeps you connected to Igbo heritage every day.

**Supported Languages:** English, Igbo
